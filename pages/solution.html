HOW TO OVERCOME YOUR INSECURITY

Defining your solution can take time. For some, it might not come as naturally or easily as they had first imagined. But don’t fret. It will come at a good time if you understand how to frame it properly. How you do this early in the idea stage can be the difference between success and failure later on. Success is clearly what everyone aims for, so what steps should you take to frame your solution successfully? You need to be able to answer the following questions: What is your solution offering? Are you actually solving the problem? Does the solution create a viable business model? Is your solution different or unique from your competitors?
